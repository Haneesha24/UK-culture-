Design Situaion

The school has identified a need to foster greater cultural awareness and appreciation among its students. To achieve this goal, the school has asked you to create a product which aims to showcase and celebrate the vibrant heritage and traditions of a specific culture. As part of the event, the students will be responsible for creating a product that will not only educate their peers but also the wider school community and parents. This product has to include the culture's rich history, unique traditions, art, music, dance, and cuisine, but it is not widely known or understood by most students at the school. The objective of the project is to create awareness and appreciation for the chosen among the school community. By doing so, the project aims to promote cultural diversity, understanding, and empathy, fostering an inclusive and respectful school environment.
